DIY Fun at the Wye Valley Youth Hostel

  • We have exclusive use of the property on a “bed only” basis, with no YHA staff present
  • The Youth Hostel is situated in 25 acres of land, a mile and a half from the nearest village, Goodrich, and 7 miles from Ross-on-Wye
  • We have to bring our own food, do our own catering in the hostel's kitchen, and bring our own drinks - all of which the hash will organise and include in the package price
  • Sleeping accommodation - female & male dormitories
