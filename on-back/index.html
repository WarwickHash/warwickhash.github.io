Managed Return to Hashing

As of August 1st, we will return to “hashing” with care...

  • All attendees must travel individually, or with members of their household
  • On arrival, please maintain a greater than 2-metre gap between yourself and any others
  • Depending on numbers, we will split attendees into groups of six people
  • Groups of six will head out from the start location at one minute intervals
  • A trail will be laid, and the first group may kick-out the trail (or leave this for the hare who will be in the final group)
  • Groups should endeavour to remain separate at the end of the trail
  • Individuals may purchase drinks from the pub, following any guidance that the establishment has in place
  • Attendees should bring/use their own individual hand sanitiser, if they wish to
  • There should be no sharing of drink containers
  • Hash fees will be collected and used to subsidise down-downs for the circle
  • It is recommended that attendees go straight home after the event

It is assumed that by attending you have read, agreed, and are willing to abide by these guidelines for the health of all involved.
